ABOUT

Dominance is a guild started on May 2nd, 2014 as a private guild. Originally called Bravo, we reached level 100 on July 3rd, 2020, later overtaking Rebel to become the #1 guild on January 14th, 2022.


Recently on August 31 2022, we became the first guild to reach level 200. Over the years, we've been through a lot of ups and down but we never give up and come out stronger every time.
