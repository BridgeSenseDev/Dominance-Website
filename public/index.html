ABOUT

Matrix is a guild started on 2nd May, 2014 originally called Bravo created as a private guild. In late 2016, we decided to revamp and open up our guild for everyone, changing our name to Matrix in December 2019.


After the guild update, the guild grew rapidly, becoming one of the first guilds to reach level 100, later passing Rebel to become the #1 overall guild in hypixel on 14th January, 2022!
